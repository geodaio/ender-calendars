How It Works

Introduction

Ender is designed For Students, By Students, providing a seamless way to organize your academic, work, and personal schedules. This guide explains how to use Ender effectively to stay on top of your tasks and deadlines.

Features

  • Sub-Calendars: Create separate calendars for classes, work, and personal life. Toggle their visibility as needed.
  • Event Management: Add, edit, and delete events with ease. Tailor each event with details like time, location, and descriptions.
  • Custom Views: Switch between daily, weekly, and monthly views to find the format that works best for you.
  • Personalization: Customize colors, fonts, and layouts to match your style.
  • Reminders: Set notifications for important deadlines and events.
  • Sync Across Devices: Access your calendar anytime, anywhere by logging into your account on any device.

FAQs

Q: Can I share my calendar with others?
A: Currently, Ender is designed for individual use, but future updates may include sharing capabilities.

Q: What happens if I forget my password?
A: Use the "Forgot Password" link on the login page to reset it via your registered email.
